Across the Sand picked up where book 1 left off, and developed a lot more of this world that was hinted at in the first one. There were a lot of different ways/avenues that could be explored after the first book, and I honestly didn't see this angle coming. It was satisfying, I will read the third book when it's published, but I'm not like staying awake all night.
